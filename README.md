## Overview

**BookMyManzil** is a smart hotel booking and guest experience management system designed to support the **complete guest journey** — from discovering and booking a room to **digital check-in**, **service requests**, and **check-out**.

The platform also supports **hotel-side operations** through role-based access for reception staff, service staff, and administrators, while integrating modern AI-driven capabilities for more personalized and intelligent user experiences.

This project brings together:

- guest-facing booking workflows
- hotel operational workflows
- role-based staff management
- AI-enhanced guest interaction
- data-driven pricing support

---

## Why BookMyManzil?

Traditional hotel systems often treat booking, guest services, and hotel operations as disconnected workflows.

**BookMyManzil unifies them into one connected platform.**

### What this means
- Guests can search and book rooms through a shared platform
- Each booking is linked to a specific hotel
- Guests can then manage their stay through hotel-specific workflows
- Staff can manage bookings, check-ins, and service requests for their own hotel
- AI capabilities improve both guest interaction and decision-making

## Product Direction

BookMyManzil is being built as a **shared multi-hotel-ready platform**.

### Platform Model
Instead of building a separate application for each hotel, the system uses:

- **one shared platform**
- **one unified codebase**
- **hotel-specific data and workflows**

### How it works
1. A guest searches and books a room
2. That booking is linked to a specific hotel
3. The same platform then gives the guest access to stay-related features for that hotel:
   - check-in
   - check-out
   - service requests
4. Staff only see the records and workflows relevant to their assigned hotel and role

This allows the system to support both:
- guest-facing booking experiences
- internal hotel management workflows
